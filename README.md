<h2 align="left">About Me 👋</h2>

I'm an aspiring **AI Product Manager** with a background in **Data Science, Machine Learning, Analytics, and AI Engineering**.

I enjoy taking products from an idea to a working solution by combining product thinking with modern AI technologies. My work focuses on building practical AI systems powered by LLMs, automation, and data rather than experimental prototypes.

<h2 align="left">What I'm Working On 🚀</h2>

Currently focused on building my data science portfolio and exploring opportunities in **recommendation systems, personalization, and human behavior analysis** — particularly in finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and marketing domains.

Recent highlights:
- **[Portfolio Optimization Project](https://github.com/MylieMudaliyar/Stock-Portfolio-Optimization-using-MPT)**: Analyzed 10 stocks across 5 sectors with sophisticated backtesting that outperformed the S&P 500
- **[Inboxd](https://inboxd.lovable.app/)**: AI-powered job search assistant that gained 100+ users in 48 hours
- **[YouBuy](https://github.com/MylieMudaliyar/Multimodal-ECommerce-Search-Engine)**: Multimodal search engine for e-commerce
- **[Sales-Marketing Analytics Framework](https://github.com/MylieMudaliyar/Sales-Marketing-Analytics-Data-Driven-Customer-Intelligence)**: 9-module end-to-end analytics framework (87% LTV prediction accuracy, 83% churn detection, 98% sales forecasting R²)

<h2 align="left">Featured Projects 🎯</h2>

Most of my projects dive deep into **DATA** — check out my repositories for source code and detailed documentation. Here are some highlights:

- **[Multimodal E-Commerce Search Engine](https://github.com/MylieMudaliyar/Multimodal-ECommerce-Search-Engine)**: CLIP embeddings + LSH approximate nearest neighbor search with association rule mining

- **[Graduate Admission Predictor](https://github.com/MylieMudaliyar/UIn-GraduateAdmissionPredictor)**: ML model deployed with Streamlit
